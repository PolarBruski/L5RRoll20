Help

This sheet is designed for use with Legend of the Five Rings 4th Edition from Alterac Entertainment Group.

This help page should simply give you a very brief overview of how the sheet was intended to be used and hopefully clear up any ambiguity. Each of the sections of the sheet (represented by the buttons at the top of the page you use to switch between sections) has an entry on this page explaining a bit about how to use it.

Important This sheet enables you to play L5R on Roll20, however, it does not automatically calculate everything from all systems. Please note that most bonuses you get from school ranks, weapon training, etc, can be added by you to the sheet, but will not be calculated automatically. The sheet should provide features that conform to the L5R 4th edition ruleset.

Core

The first section of the sheet provides space to track the core stats and attributes every character has. Firstly it provides space for your 5 rings and the 10 associated traits, which will inform the rest of the rolls, skills, and weapons on the sheet. This is a very important section to get right as those ring and trait values are used in many places on the sheet!
